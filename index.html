Fun Facts about KitKat

Origin: KitKat was introduced in 1935 by Rowntree's of York, England.

Slogan: "Have a Break, Have a KitKat" is one of the most famous slogans worldwide.

Unique Flavors: In Japan, KitKat is available in flavors like matcha green tea, wasabi, and sake.
